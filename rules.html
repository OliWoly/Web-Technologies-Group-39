Double Ring

Hitting the thin outer ring doubles the segment score, while the thin inner ring triples it.

  1. Each player gets 3 darts per turn
  2. Aim for high-value segments (20, 19, 18)
  3. Score subtracts from starting total (usually 501)
  4. First to reach exactly 0 wins
  5. Must finish on a double (outer ring)
