Scoring

Scoring follows well known darts rules.

Each field corresponds to the amount of points scored when hit.
The smaller fields within each one act as a multiplier of the score.

Outer ring = X2

Inner rings = X3

Double Bull = 25

Bullseye = 50

Gameplay

Try and score as many points as possible with the three shots you are given! When you decide your round is over, or run out of shots, enter your initials/name by clicking the corresponding button, then click reset. Doing so will save your score to be viewed later. When you have decided that you and everyone else has finished playing, click send scores and view them on the scores page!
